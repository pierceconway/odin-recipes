Ham and Potato Casserole


A hearty and delicious casserole for days where you need a simple, feel-good meal

Ingredients

  • 4 cups frozen potatos
  • 1 1/2 cups cubed ham (3/4-inch pieces
  • 1 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese
  • 1 can condensed cream of chicken soup
  • 1/2 cup sour cream

Directions

  1. Heat oven to 375. Grease 2 quart glass baking dish
  2. In a large bowl, mix all ingredients; spoon into baking dish
  3. Bake 50 to 55 minutes or until bubbly and hot. Let stand 5 minutes
